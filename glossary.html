Boltzmann distribution

Probablility distribution that gives the probability of a state as a function of the state and the temperature of the system.

double contraction

Let \(\mathbf{A}\) and \(\mathbf{B}\) two second order tensors. The double contraction between \(\mathbf{A}\) and \(\mathbf{B}\) is \(\mathbf{A}:\mathbf{B}=\mathbf{A}_{ij}\mathbf{B}_{ji}\). Let \(\mathbb{C}\) a fourth order tensor. The double contraction between \(\mathbb{C}\) and \(\mathbf{A}\) is \((\mathbb{C}:\mathbf{A)_{ij}}=\mathbb{C}_{ijkl}:\mathbf{A}_{lk}\)

dyadic product

The dyadic product between two vectors \(\pmb{u}\) and \(\pmb{v}\) is \(\pmb{u}\otimes\pmb{v}=u_iv_j\).

grain

Crystalline structure differentiated by its orientation. Euler angles or quaternions are used to define this.
